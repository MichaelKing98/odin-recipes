Chicken Schnitzel

Home made, golden chicken schnitzel.

Ingredients

    Chicken breast
    Flour
    Paprika
    Eggs
    Bread crumbs
    Lemon Zest

Instructions

    1. Pound chicken breast to 1/4-inch thick pieces.
    2. Season all chicken with salt and pepper
    3. Crack 2 eggs into a bowl
    4. Mix bread crumbs, lemon zest and paprika into a bowl
    5. Put flour into a bowl
    6. Cover chicken pieces with flour
    7. Take chicken from flour into egg mixture
    8. Take chicken from egg mixture and cover with breadcrumb mixture
    9. Repeat steps 6-8 until all chicken is complete
    10. Bake in oven until chicken is golden brown, flipping if neccesary.
